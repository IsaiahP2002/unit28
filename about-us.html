About Us

We at Real News are all about providing people with accurate and up-to-date news, to combat and prevent people from being misled and believing this that aren't true. It is not always easy to spot and realise when you are exposed to fake news, and if you take it at face value, it could be a bad thing.

All of what we post here is 100% true and factual, to prevent people from being misled so fear not.

Real News was founded by Isaiah Patterson in 2020 after he did some research on how frequent and prevalent fake news is in our society today, and it became his goal and aim to provide people with real and accurate news.

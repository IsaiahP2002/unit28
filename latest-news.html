End of Lockdown in Sight?

This week Prime Minister Boris Johnson revealed his plans for ending the current lockdown and releasing the country. He outlined a four step plan to end restrictions by 21st June 2021. The first step was to open all schools on March 8th, with students needing to be tested and wear a facemask. Following this, he said all non essential shops like hairdressers and barbers could reopen on April 12th. Step three involves allowing six people or 2+ households to mix indoors. Finally, he hoped that he would be able to reopen every remaining closed door of the economy on June 21st. (Sky News)
